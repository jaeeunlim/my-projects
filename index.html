    Welcome to Esther's Website

    I have a BS degree in Mechanical Engineering and Robotics from Carnegie Mellon University. I am very passionate about building and programming robots that can do cool and complex tasks. When it comes to building or programming something, my passion and enthusiasm never fail until I see it accomplished and completed. I have done numerous projects, every single one of which I greatly enjoyed and into which I put a lot of effort. You can access the links to my projects from the top navigation.

    I am particularly interested in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, automation/mechanical/robotic systems, and educational robots.

    Additionally, I enjoy painting, sewing, playing ukulele, hiking, camping, and studying the Bible. My ultimate goal is to serve others with my time, energy, skills, and knowledge.

    - Software: SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor, Arduino, MS Office
