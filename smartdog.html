    Smart Dog Toy

    For Mechatronics capstone project, in a team of five, I built a smart dog toy that can be controlled remotely, shoot treats, and has an onboard camera to monitor pet dogs at home. I designed the body and assembly structure of the overall system. I have contributed to developing and finalizing ideas for the launching and loading mechanism. I was the leader of the team, organizing weekly meetings, developing timeline for the entire project, and ensuring consistent progress of each team member. You can access the project report and poster.
